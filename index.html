IPL Fantasy Faceoff

IPL Fantasy Faceoff is a full-stack web app built around a season-long IPL fantasy rivalry between two friends. It combines a live scoreboard, team archives, matchday posts, reader profiles, guest comments, replies, mentions, and social-style reactions to turn a private fantasy league into an interactive story that fans can follow throughout the season.

React | Node.js | Next.js | Cloudinary | Vercel
